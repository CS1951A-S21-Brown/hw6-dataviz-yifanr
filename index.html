4. People who have somewhat weak vision should still be okay since I refrained from using light colors in my bar graph with a white background. I also don't have anything color-coded, so no information is really hidden by color.
If the user is blind, it would be quite difficult for them to visualize the data even though there are captions and labels that the reader could use. I could improve upon this by adding summaries of the data under the graphs and providing the key points, like "the top 3 genres were... with ... entries respectively". This would make it easier for blind people to get the main points of the data. I got this from one of the readings and thought it was a good idea. Additionally, I could perhaps learn to add thin black borders around the bars to make the contrast even better while still keeping aesthetics.
